Space Samples

Space Samples is a service for those who import products from China and want to have a showroom in a strategic location at a low cost. The idea is good, but it needed to be explained well.

There were some exchanges of emails and sketches until reaching the final explanation, which had versions for each medium that would be used – email, mobile, print. I wrote the copy, and made infographics and illustrations.

I started editing the text the client had sent me. Then, the sketches began to adjust the visual form of the explanation and the tone of the illustrations.

I also made maps and infographics to explain the features of the company's facilities.
