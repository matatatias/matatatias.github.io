Paraná State GDP Visualizations 

The abundance of data, in some situations, hinders rather than helps. In this case, I took the data from the reporter's hands to explore it visually and choose the best visualizations for a full-page infographic. After selecting the views, the reporter prepared texts to present each one.

The tools used included vector tools – Freehand and Illustrator –, spreadsheets, Processing scripts, QGIS for processing the maps, and some obscure software to proportionally modify the areas of the municipalities.
